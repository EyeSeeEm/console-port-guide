Government Grants

German Federal Games Funding (Computerspieleförderung)

Up to 50% Funding Non-Repayable Grant

Germany's federal games funding program provides non-repayable grants of up to 50% for game development. Since 2019, over 640 projects have received 220+ million in total funding. Applications reopened December 30, 2024 after a 20-month freeze.

Funding Amounts
Category Details
Minimum Project Volume 300,000
Maximum Funding 2 million (standard) / 8 million (large projects)
Large Companies Up to 25% of eligible costs
SMEs (<250 employees, <50M turnover) Up to 45% of eligible costs
Startups (<3 years old) Up to 50% of eligible costs (+5% bonus)
Key Requirements
  • German presence required: Registered office, permanent establishment, or branch in Germany
  • Cultural test: Game must pass EU cultural test based on content and cultural background
  • Age rating: USK label required for all public builds (even Steam/trade shows)
  • Accessibility: Must include low-barrier options (subtitles, font sizes, etc.)
  • No dark patterns: Gambling simulation and manipulative designs excluded
  • Own funds: At least 50% of costs must come from company resources
  • Mandatory consultation: Must consult with DLR project agency before applying
  • Project not started: Cannot fund work already begun (unless "early start" approved)
Application Timeline
Step 1: Consultation with DLR

Mandatory preliminary discussion with project agency (required before application)

Step 2: Prepare Documentation

Project description, financing plan, SME declaration, cultural test, submission checklist

Step 3: Submit via easy-Online

Digital-only submission. Next deadline: December 31, 2025

Step 4: Evaluation

Based on innovation, creativity, cultural test, economic viability, team qualifications

Step 5: Project Start

Projects can start from October 1, 2025 (or earlier with approved exemption)

Self-Funded Porting

Console Porting Costs & Timeline

If platform funding isn't available, here's what to expect for self-funded console ports.

Cost Estimates
Scope Cost Range Timeline
Small indie to one platform Mid five-figures ($30K-$60K) 3-4 months
Indie to next-gen (PS5/XSX) Upper five-figures ~2 months
Indie to multiple platforms Low six-figures 4-6 months
Complex/AAA games Low seven-figures 10+ months
Factors Affecting Cost
  • Platform similarity: PC to Xbox is cheaper (similar architecture); PS/Nintendo costs more
  • Game engine: Unity/Unreal have better multi-platform support than proprietary engines
  • Combined ports: PS4+PS5 together = ~4 months (vs. 5 months separate)
Payment Models
One-Time Fee
Pay upfront for development. You keep all revenue.
Revenue Share
Reduced/no upfront cost. Studio takes ongoing percentage.
